# ISO 42001 AIMS Readiness Checklist

Detailed punch list per ISO 42001 clause + Annex A control area. For each item: evidence required, common gap, audit-day expectation.

---

## Clause 4 — Context of the Organization

| Sub-clause | Item | Evidence |
|------------|------|----------|
| 4.1 | Understanding org + context | Strategic context document |
| 4.2 | Interested parties + requirements | Stakeholder analysis |
| 4.3 | Scope of AIMS | Documented scope (systems/products/services in/out) |
| 4.4 | AIMS structure | AIMS map / overview |

**Audit expectation:** auditor verifies that scope is appropriate (not too narrow to exclude key systems; not too broad to be unmanageable).

---

## Clause 5 — Leadership

| Sub-clause | Item | Evidence |
|------------|------|----------|
| 5.1 | Leadership + commitment | Management commitment statement; resource allocation evidence |
| 5.2 | AI policy | Signed AI policy |
| 5.3 | Roles, responsibilities, authorities | RACI matrix; appointment letters |

**Audit expectation:** AI policy reviewed for completeness (ethics, accountability, transparency); leadership commitment via interviews.

---

## Clause 6 — Planning

| Sub-clause | Item | Evidence |
|------------|------|----------|
| 6.1 | Actions to address risks + opportunities | Risk register with AI-specific risks |
| 6.1.2 | AI risk assessment | Per-system risk assessment |
| 6.1.3 | AI risk treatment | Treatment plans per risk |
| 6.1.4 | AI Impact Assessment (AIIA) | Per-system AIIA |
| 6.2 | AI objectives + planning to achieve | Documented objectives + measurable targets |
| 6.3 | Planning of changes | Change management process for AIMS |

**Audit expectation:** AIIA exists for each AI system in scope; treatment plans show progress.

## Pre-audit checklist (week before)

- [ ] AI inventory current
- [ ] AIIA per in-scope system completed + recent
- [ ] AI policy signed within last 12 months
- [ ] Management review held within last 12 months
- [ ] Internal audit completed for relevant period
- [ ] Training records current per role
- [ ] AI lifecycle records per system (design, validation, deployment, monitoring)
- [ ] Data documentation per system
- [ ] Third-party AI vendor list + assessments
- [ ] Document control: all relevant procedures current

---

## Audit-day operations

### Front room

- AIMS-trained representative accompanies auditor
- Document retrieval support available
- Witness scheduling coordinated

### Back room

- Cross-functional team available (AI engineering, data, security, legal)
- Documents ready by topic area
- Evidence packets pre-organized

### Daily debrief

- Topics covered
- Observations + planned follow-ups
- Next-day preparation

## Cheat sheet

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| First step? | Confirm AI system inventory completeness |
| Most important Annex A area? | A.5 (Impact Assessment) + A.6 (Lifecycle) |
| Internal audit timing? | Within 12 months of certification audit |
| Management review timing? | Within 12 months; annual minimum |
| Stage 1 vs Stage 2? | Stage 1 = docs review; Stage 2 = operational evidence |
| Surveillance audit cadence? | Annual; reduced scope |
| Certification term? | 3 years before re-certification |
| Most common gap? | AI inventory incomplete; AIIA missing for some systems |
