# Onboarding 1:1 Question Bank

Question banks for new-PM 1:1s, organized by partner type. Pick 5-7 per conversation; don't run through all of them.

---

## With your manager (week 1)

**Set-up:**

- "What does success look like for me at 30 days? 90 days? 6 months?"
- "How do you prefer to be updated -- weekly written, async, sync?"
- "What's the escalation threshold? When should I bring you in?"
- "What did my predecessor do well? What did they do poorly?"
- "Who in the org do I most need to build trust with?"
- "What would derail me in the first 6 months?"
- "What is the one thing you'd most want from this role in 90 days?"

**Style:**

- "How do you give feedback? How would you like me to give you feedback?"
- "How do you prefer to make decisions -- consensus, your call, my call?"
- "When you say 'urgent', what does that mean?"

**Calibration:**

- "What level are you expecting me to operate at? What does that bar look like?"
- "Who else's calibration of me matters in the room?"
- "What's the worst-case scenario for my first quarter?"

---

## With your manager (week 4: "What I'm learning" review)

- "Here's my read on the STARS situation [share]. Do you agree?"
- "Here are the top 3 things I want to preserve. Anything missing?"
- "Here are the top 3 risks I've identified. Anything to add?"
- "What questions am I not asking that I should be?"

---

## With your skip-level

- "How does this product fit into the company's broader strategy?"
- "What does the bar for our team look like vs. peer teams?"
- "Where do you think this area should be in 12 months?"
- "What's the biggest constraint on us today?"
- "What would change your view of how this area is performing?"
- "What's a story you've heard about our area recently -- good or bad?"

---

## With your engineering lead

- "What does your team most need from a PM?"
- "What did my predecessor get right? Wrong?"
- "What are you most worried about right now?"
- "Where do we have technical debt that's actively slowing us?"
- "Are there projects you've wanted to do but can't make the case for?"
- "How do you make scoping calls when timelines slip?"
- "What's the team's appetite for risk right now?"

---

## With your design lead

- "What does great design partnership with PM look like to you?"
- "What did my predecessor get right? Wrong?"
- "What are the user problems that have nagged you that we haven't tackled?"
- "Where do we under-invest in design quality?"
- "What customer research do we have? What do we need more of?"

---

## With each engineer (immediate team)

- "What's your tenure here? What did you do before?"
- "What's the most interesting thing you're working on right now?"
- "What's the most frustrating thing about your work right now?"
- "What would you change about how we operate?"
- "What's a customer pain you wish we'd address?"
- "Anything you'd want me to know about how this team works?"

---

## With each designer (immediate team)

(Mostly same as engineers, plus:)

- "What's the design culture here -- what's valued, what's not?"
- "What customer research have you been part of?"
- "What's a UX problem you've raised that hasn't been picked up?"

---

## With adjacent PMs

- "What's the biggest area of overlap between our products?"
- "How do we typically handle cross-team dependencies?"
- "What's your team focused on this quarter?"
- "What should I know about working with leadership here?"
- "Anyone in the org I should reach out to specifically?"

---

## With Sales / GTM

- "What's the top customer ask you can't satisfy today?"
- "What's the top objection in deals?"
- "Where do you think our product is strongest? Weakest?"
- "Who are 2-3 customers I should talk to?"
- "What's a competitor that's winning more deals than they should?"

---

## With Customer Success

- "What's the top reason for churn?"
- "What feature lands best with customers in the first 30 days?"
- "Where do customers get stuck during onboarding?"
- "Who are your most engaged customers? Most frustrated?"
- "What's the most common 'why doesn't it do X' question?"

---

## With Support

- "What are your top 3 ticket categories?"
- "What product issue takes the longest to resolve?"
- "What's a bug you've raised that you wish we'd fixed?"
- "Where does our documentation fail customers?"

---

## With Marketing

- "How do we currently position this product?"
- "What does positioning need to look like in 12 months?"
- "Who's our ideal customer? Who's not?"
- "What's launching when?"
- "What's a competitor we should be talking about more?"

---

## With Data / Analytics

- "What's the metric tree for our area?"
- "What dashboards do you trust most?"
- "What data do we wish we had?"
- "What's an A/B test result that surprised you recently?"
- "What's a metric we report that you don't trust?"

---

## With Legal / Privacy

- "What current product behavior worries you?"
- "What's coming up in regulation that I should know about?"
- "What's the escalation process for compliance questions?"
- "What's a recent compliance close call?"

---

## Closing questions for every 1:1

These three close every conversation well:

1. "Is there anything you wish I'd asked you?"
2. "Who else should I be talking to about this?"
3. "Anything I can do for you in the next two weeks?"
