## Conversation goals

For each Tier 1 and Tier 2 1:1, aim to learn:

- **What you do** -- their role and accountabilities
- **Top 3 priorities** for the quarter
- **What's going well** they want to preserve
- **What's broken** or hard to do today
- **How they want to work with me** -- communication frequency, decision involvement
- **Who else should I be talking to**

For Tier 1 specifically, also discuss:

- Working style preferences (sync vs. async, written vs. verbal, etc.)
- Escalation thresholds
- What success looks like for the partnership in 6 months
