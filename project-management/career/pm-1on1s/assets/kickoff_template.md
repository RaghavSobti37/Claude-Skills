## Purpose statement (1 sentence, share aloud)

> "Our 1:1 is for ___________________________________________________."

Examples by type:

- Manager: "Our 1:1 is for me to bring you the judgment calls and feedback that don't fit in writing, and for you to give me direction and growth feedback."
- EM partner: "Our 1:1 is for coordinating on the team's operating cadence, surfacing tension early, and growing our partnership."
- Direct report: "Our 1:1 is for you -- your topics, your growth, your blockers -- with feedback flowing both ways."
- Cross-functional: "Our 1:1 is for mutual context, alignment on priorities, and early-warning signals from each side."
