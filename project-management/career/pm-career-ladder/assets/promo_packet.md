## Checklist before submission

- [ ] Each impact bullet has a quantified outcome
- [ ] Each impact bullet names "I" not "we" for the central action
- [ ] Rubric mapping is concrete (no "I demonstrated leadership" -- show a specific behavior)
- [ ] Quotes come from at least 3 different functions
- [ ] Growth narrative includes both strengths and a still-developing area
- [ ] "What's next" lands at the proposed level, not the current one
- [ ] Manager has reviewed and signed off
- [ ] Skip-level has seen at least the impact summary
