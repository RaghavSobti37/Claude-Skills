# Beta Recruitment Scripts

Three scripts, one per cohort. Each leads with the participant's pain, not the feature. Adjust tone to match your brand voice.

---

## Cohort 1: Friends (Internal / Advisors)

**Channel:** Slack DM, in-person ask, or internal email.

> Subject: Quick favor -- 2-week beta of [Feature]
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> We are about to open a closed beta for [Feature], which helps [target user] [outcome]. Before we touch any real customers, I want to break it in private with a small group of people I trust to give me brutal feedback.
>
> The ask: 15-20 minutes of poking at it this week, plus reading the Monday digest for two weeks. If you find something embarrassing, I owe you a coffee.
>
> Can I add you on [date]?
>
> [Name]

**Screening:** None. Friends-cohort participants self-select. Cap at 25% of total cohort.

---

## Cohort 2: Family (Existing Customers / Design Partners)

**Channel:** Email from CSM with PM cc'd, or Slack Connect.

> Subject: Early access to [Feature] -- want in?
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> Based on our last call about [their pain point], I think you might want first look at something we are about to release in closed beta.
>
> [Feature] is designed to [outcome tied to their pain]. We are inviting 15 design partners to use it for the next 6 weeks before general availability. In exchange, we ask for:
>
> - 30 minutes/week to try it out
> - One 30-minute call in week 3 for feedback
> - Permission to ask if we can quote you at launch (we always ask first)
>
> In return, you get:
>
> - First production use of the feature
> - A direct line to the PM ([name]) and engineering lead ([name])
> - [Pricing commitment: e.g., "Locked rate of $X/user for the first 12 months post-GA"]
>
> The beta starts [date]. Want in?
>
> [Name]

**Screening Questions (before activation):**

1. What does your week look like right now in terms of [problem area]?
2. What tool or workaround do you use today?
3. If [Feature] saved you [time/headcount/cost], what would you do with that?
4. Who else on your team would benefit?
5. Are you OK with us recording sessions for internal use only?

## Cohort 3: Fanatics (Cold Outreach)

**Channel:** LinkedIn DM, cold email, community DM. Lead with the problem, not the company.

> Subject: [Their pain point], one question
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> Saw your post / talk / comment about [specific pain point]. Quick question: how are you handling [pain] today?
>
> Asking because we just built something for exactly this. It is in closed beta -- 15 spots, 6 weeks, no charge. I am screening for fit before I open invites.
>
> If you are open to a 15-min call this week to see if it fits, here is my calendar: [link].
>
> Either way, happy to share what we learned about [pain] from talking to ~50 people who have it.
>
> [Name]
> [Role, Company]

**Screening Call (15 min, before activation):**

1. Walk me through how you handle [pain] today, step by step.
2. How often does this come up? (weekly / monthly / quarterly)
3. What have you tried before? Why did it not work?
4. If we could give you [outcome], how would that change your week?
5. Who else owns this with you?
6. If this works for you in 6 weeks, what would happen next? (Are you the buyer? Who is?)

**Disqualifiers (politely decline):**

- The pain is not currently active for them.
- They are evaluating 5+ alternatives in parallel and just want to compare.
- They are a known competitor or competitor-adjacent.
- They are unable to commit 30 min/week.

---

## Follow-Up Cadence

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 0 | Send the outreach |
| Day 3 | Nudge if no response: "Bumping this in case it got buried" |
| Day 7 | Final follow-up: "Closing recruitment Friday -- still want in?" |
| Day 8 | Mark as declined; do not chase further |

## Activation Email (All Cohorts)

> Subject: You are in -- [Feature] beta starts [date]
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> You are confirmed for the [Feature] closed beta. Here is what to expect:
>
> **What you will get:**
> - Access at [link] starting [date]
> - The Monday digest with what shipped, known issues, and what to try this week
> - Tuesday office hours (30 min, optional) at [link]
> - A 1:1 with me in week 3 ([calendar link])
>
> **What we ask:**
> - 30 min/week to try the suggested tasks
> - Reply to the Monday digest with anything broken or surprising
> - Sign the attached one-page NDA before [date]
>
> **Headline goal for this beta:**
> [State the outcome, e.g., "We are testing whether you can [outcome] in [time]."]
>
> Looking forward to it.
>
> [Name]
> PM, [Company]
