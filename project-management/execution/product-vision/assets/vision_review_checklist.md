## Category 1: Inspiring (score 1-5)

> Would engineers, designers, PMs, and prospective hires *want* to bring this vision into being?

- [ ] Does the vision describe a state of the world that the team would be proud to create?
- [ ] Would a strong candidate read this and want to interview?
- [ ] Does it survive a "so what?" test -- if a team member asked "so what?", is the answer compelling?
- [ ] Does it make the team's current work feel meaningful (connected to the destination)?

**Score:** [1-5]

**Notes:** [observations, weak points]

---

## Category 2: Concrete (score 1-5)

> Is the vision specific enough to direct decisions today?

- [ ] Does it name a specific customer (segment, job, or context)?
- [ ] Does it name a specific outcome (measurable in some form)?
- [ ] Could a roadmap decision be evaluated against the vision? ("Does this Q3 work move us toward [vision component]?")
- [ ] Could two team members read the vision and reach the same conclusion about a priority call?

**Score:** [1-5]

**Notes:** [observations, weak points]

---

## Category 3: Durable (score 1-5)

> Will the vision survive 5-10 years of change?

- [ ] Does it survive a major technology shift (e.g., a new AI paradigm, a new compute model)?
- [ ] Does it survive a re-positioning in the market?
- [ ] Is it free of feature names, version numbers, or specific implementations that will retire?
- [ ] Is the underlying customer job stable over time (or is it tied to a trend that may fade)?

**Score:** [1-5]

**Notes:** [observations, weak points]

---

## Category 4: Differentiated (score 1-5)

> Could the vision belong to a competitor?

- [ ] If you replaced the company name, would the vision still feel specific?
- [ ] Does it name what makes our path different from the alternatives?
- [ ] Does it survive the test: "would any competitor write this same vision?"
- [ ] Is there a clear, named obstacle that our product solves better than alternatives?

**Score:** [1-5]

**Notes:** [observations, weak points]

---

## Category 5: Memorable (score 1-5)

> Can the team repeat the vision from memory?

- [ ] Can a new team member repeat the vision (or its one-sentence summary) after one read?
- [ ] Is the core sentence under 30 words?
- [ ] Does it survive translation between formats (Pichler -> Moore -> Raskin)?
- [ ] Is it free of buzzwords and jargon?

**Score:** [1-5]

**Notes:** [observations, weak points]
