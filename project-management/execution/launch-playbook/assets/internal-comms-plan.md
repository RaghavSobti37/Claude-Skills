| Audience | What they need to know | Owner | Channel |
|----------|------------------------|-------|---------|

## 2. Pre-Launch Cadence

### T-30: Kickoff Memo (to all stakeholders)

> Subject: [Feature] launch -- T-30 kickoff
>
> Team,
>
> We are launching [Feature] on [date]. This is the kickoff memo.
>
> **What it is:** [One paragraph; the positioning brief.]
>
> **Who it is for:** [Target segment.]
>
> **Why now:** [Strategic context.]
>
> **Key dates:**
> - T-21: Positioning brief signed
> - T-14: Marketing assets drafted
> - T-7: Dry-run + readiness review
> - T-3: Go/no-go
> - T-0: Launch
>
> **Workstream owners:** [Link to RACI]
>
> **Where to ask questions:** [#launch-feature-name Slack channel]
>
> [Comms Owner]

### T-14: Sales Preview

> Subject: Preview of [Feature] -- sales enablement at T-7
>
> Sales team,
>
> [Feature] launches on [date]. Here is what you need to know to start:
>
> - **Positioning (one line):** [Headline]
> - **Pricing:** [$X per Y]
> - **Target accounts:** [Segment + named-account list to come]
> - **Battle card vs:** [Competitor 1, Competitor 2]
>
> Full enablement session: [date], [time], [link]. Attendance required.
>
> Materials in [Drive/Notion folder].
>
> [PMM]

### T-7: All-Hands Preview

Brief slide (3 minutes) at the weekly company all-hands:

- What is launching, when, and who it is for.
- Why this matters strategically.
- How to talk about it externally (social sharing guidance).
- Where to learn more (talking points doc).

## 4. Post-Launch Cadence

### T+1: Day-1 Memo

> Subject: [Feature] launch -- Day 1 recap
>
> **Numbers:** [traffic, signups, activation, errors]
> **Press coverage:** [count and tone]
> **Top support theme:** [theme + count]
> **Risks:** [list]
> **Next 24 hours:** [planned actions]

### T+7: Week-1 Memo

Mirrors Day 1 with week-over-week trend and the week-1 retro headline finding.

### T+30: 30-Day Decision Memo

Three sections:
1. **What we measured:** numbers vs forecast.
2. **What we learned:** themes, surprises, customer quotes.
3. **What we are doing next:** invest more / sustain / iterate / sunset, with named owner.

---

## 5. Talking Points (for all employees)

A one-page doc covering:

- **What it is** (one sentence)
- **Who it is for** (one sentence)
- **Why we built it** (one sentence)
- **What it replaces or improves** (one sentence)
- **Pricing** (or "see pricing page")
- **Where to learn more** (link)
- **What NOT to say** (anything outside this doc; route inbound to [channel])
