# JTBD 2-Hour Mini-Workshop Agenda

**Format:** 2-hour validation workshop
**Output:** Validated top 3 jobs and aligned vocabulary
**Participants:** 4-8 (product trio + 2-3 customer-facing roles)
**Required pre-work:** 2 switch interview transcripts read by all participants
**Facilitator:** PM or Researcher
**Materials:** Miro/FigJam board or printed canvases; timer; transcripts

---

## 0:00 - 0:10 | Welcome + JTBD Primer (10 min)

- Round-robin intros (15 sec each)
- Workshop goal: validate or update the top 3 jobs for [segment]
- 60-second JTBD primer:
  - "We're not asking 'who is the customer.' We're asking 'what is the customer trying to do.'"
  - Definitions: job, outcome, force of progress
- Ground rules: no solutions yet, no demographic framing, all output must trace to customer voice

---

## 0:10 - 0:25 | Customer Voice Grounding (15 min)

- Read aloud Switch Interview Summary #1 (5 min)
- Read aloud Switch Interview Summary #2 (5 min)
- Open Slack/board for participants to write notable quotes (5 min)

---

## 0:25 - 0:45 | Job Statement Drafting (20 min)

- Individual silent writing (10 min): each participant writes 3-5 candidate job statements using the format:
  ```
  [Customer] is trying to [verb] [object] when [situation]
  so that [outcome / progress].
  ```
- Round-robin: each person reads one favorite (10 min)

---

## 0:45 - 0:55 | Dot Vote on Top 3 Jobs (10 min)

- Cluster duplicates and near-duplicates (5 min)
- Each participant gets 3 dots; vote for the most important jobs (3 min)
- Tally and announce top 3 (2 min)

---

## 0:55 - 1:25 | Decompose Top 3 into Outcomes (30 min)

- For each of the top 3 jobs (~10 min each):
  - Brainstorm 3-5 desired outcomes in Ulwick format:
    ```
    Minimize [time / effort / likelihood / number / cost] of
    [verb] [object] [context].
    ```
  - Quality check: does it pass the outcome statement quality test?
- Capture the full outcome list

---

## 1:25 - 1:45 | Forces-of-Progress Quick Map (20 min)

- For the #1 job only (the others can be done as homework):
  - Push: what is bad about the current state? (5 min)
  - Pull: what is attractive about the alternative? (5 min)
  - Anxiety: what worries customers about switching? (5 min)
  - Habit: what ties them to the current way? (5 min)

---

## 1:45 - 2:00 | Synthesis + Next Steps (15 min)

- Review the artifacts produced:
  - Top 3 jobs
  - 9-15 outcomes (3-5 per job)
  - Forces-of-progress quick map for job #1
- Assign owners for the top 3 outcomes (named owner + 30-day next step)
- Schedule a 30-day check-in
- Define what gets handed off downstream (PRD, roadmap, experiments)

---

## Post-Workshop Deliverable

Within 48 hours, facilitator produces:

- One-page summary with top 3 jobs and 9-15 outcomes
- Forces map for job #1
- Owner + next-step list
- Link to source switch interview transcripts (for traceability)

Hand off to one of: `discovery/identify-assumptions/`, `discovery/brainstorm-experiments/`, `execution/create-prd/`, `execution/outcome-roadmap/`.
