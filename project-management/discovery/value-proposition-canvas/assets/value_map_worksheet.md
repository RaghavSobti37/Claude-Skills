## Validation Checklist

Before declaring problem-solution fit:

- [ ] Every Essential pain reliever maps to a top-3 pain
- [ ] Every Essential gain creator maps to a top-3 gain
- [ ] At least one gain creator addresses an Unexpected (delighter) gain
- [ ] Coverage audit complete; uncovered items have a decision and an owner
- [ ] Sales translation completed for every Essential item
- [ ] Evidence column filled for every pain reliever and gain creator
- [ ] Canvas reviewed by Product Trio (PM + Design + Eng) and at least one customer
